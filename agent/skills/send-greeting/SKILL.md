# send-greeting

Draft short greeting messages for the health assistant.

## Workflow

1. Identify the scenario: welcome, daily greeting, follow-up, holiday, or group greeting.
2. Identify the tone: warm, professional, casual, upbeat, or respectful.
3. Decide whether the greeting should be generic or personalized.
4. Draft 1 to 3 options unless a single fixed message is requested.
5. Keep the message brief and copy-ready.

## Output requirements

- Prefer Chinese unless another language is requested.
- Keep default greetings to 1 or 2 sentences.
- Avoid exaggerated enthusiasm, emoji spam, or sales language unless explicitly requested.
- Use natural continuation instead of ceremonial wording in ongoing chats.
